CONCLUSION

1. Customers preferred "Regular Air" mode of delivery in SHIP MODE.

2. "Office supplies" were ordered most through "Regular Air" mode of delivery , while "Furniture" were ordered most through "Delivery Truck" mode of delivery.

3. In "Customer Segment" most of the orders came from the "CORPORATE" sector , while the lowest orders came from "SMALL BUSINESSES" sector.

4. "Office supplies" got most orders in the "Product Category" section in which "PAPER" was the most ordered item in the office supplies category.

5. In "TECHNOLOGY" section , the most ordered item was "TELEHPONES and COMMUNICATIONS" , while the "OFFICE FURNISHINGS" was the most ordered item in "FURNITURE" section.

6. The numbers of Orders were gradually increased year by year.

7. The "TECHNOLOGY" section received less orders but the margin of profit in "TECHNOLOGY" section was higher as compared to "OFFICE SUPPLIES" or "FURNITURE".

8. TOP 5 states which saw most sales were "CALIFORNIA" , "TEXAS" , "ILLINOIS" , "NEW YORK" and "FLORIDA".

RECOMMENDATION

1. In Product Category "FURNITURE" section saw the less number of orders and profit margin . so, the "FURNITURE" category needs to be worked on more.

2. In "STATES" section "CALIFORNIA" got most number of orders. so, the company should focus on giving more offers and discounts to the customers living in california for more busniess growth and development.

3. "REGULAR AIR" mode of delivery should be given more priority because of the most number of orders.
